# Policy stance (hybrid):
#   • ALLOW AI search bots that drive inbound citation traffic
#     (Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, Gemini, Apple Intelligence, Meta AI).
#     llms.txt at /llms.txt provides their reading map.
#   • DISALLOW pure training-data harvesters that give no traffic in
#     return (Common Crawl, Bytespider, Amazonbot).
#
# Why: we want top-of-funnel discoverability inside AI search products
# without donating the corpus to general-purpose model training.
